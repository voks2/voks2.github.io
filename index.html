BIO

Vojin Mitrović has a background in media, starting in the early 2000s as a press clipping agent and later as a journalist for a daily print newspaper in Belgrade, Serbia. In 2005, he became involved in the "You’re Right!" project as a research associate and author for the Belgrade Center for Human Rights Youth Group, contributing to the third edition of its legal guide for high school students.

In 2006, he co-founded the non-profit organization "You’re Right!", which expanded on the initial project. Vojin assumed the role of Executive Editor for the website upravusi.rs, an online platform publishing legal guides for various audiences. He coordinated the legal and research teams and created a specialized collaboration platform, enabling journalists, researchers, and external experts to work together to produce articles and documentation simplifying various legal and procedural matters.

From 2012 to 2014, he worked as a Community Manager for Fund B92, a renowned culture and philanthropy organization in Serbia. He administered websites and social media for the regional youth web series #JustSaying and helped coordinate aid distribution following the 2014 Balkan floods.

Since 2015, Vojin has been employed as a Web Manager at the Slavko Ćuruvija Foundation, a nonprofit dedicated to promoting press freedom in Serbia with a strong focus on the safety of journalists. Vojin is responsible for planning, producing, and deploying content for the Foundation's websites, social media, and other platforms. He also serves as the technical manager for the Foundation's online media outlet, Cenzolovka.rs.
